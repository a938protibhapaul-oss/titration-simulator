Procedure

  • Standardization of NaOH:
  • Pipette out 10 mL of 0.1 N oxalic acid into a conical flask.
  • Add one drop of phenolphthalein indicator.
  • Titrate with NaOH solution from the burette.
  • Stop when the solution turns very light pink.
  • Note the final burette reading.

  • Estimation of HCl:
  • Pipette out 10 mL of HCl solution into a conical flask.
  • Add one drop of phenolphthalein indicator.
  • Titrate with the standardized NaOH solution.
  • Stop when a very light pink colour appears.
  • Note the final burette reading.

  • Calculation:
  • Use the relation V₁S₁ = V₂S₂.
  • V₁ = volume of acid
  • S₁ = strength of acid
  • V₂ = volume of base
  • S₂ = strength of base
